關於我們About Us

影音狂熱成立於2006年，是一家專業從事影音產品銷售與服務的公司。我們提供各種高品質的音響、影音設備和配件，以及相關的技術支援和售後服務。 我們的產品範圍包括家庭影院、專業音響、多媒體投影、音響配件、線材等。我們的產品來自全球知名品牌，如Bose、Sony、Denon、Marantz、JBL等，並且我們也提供定制化產品和方案，以滿足客戶多樣化的需求。 影音狂熱的使命是為客戶提供最好的產品和服務，以及最具競爭力的價格。我們的專業團隊擁有豐富的行業經驗和專業知識，可以為客戶提供專業的建議和解決方案。 我們致力於成為影音產品銷售和服務的領先企業，並為客戶提供一流的產品和服務體驗。


Sound Oasis was founded in 2006, and is a professional company specializing in the sales and service of audio and video products. We offer a wide range of high-quality audio, video equipment and accessories, as well as related technical support and after-sales service. Our product range includes home theater, professional audio, multimedia projection, audio accessories, cables and more. Our products come from globally renowned brands such as Bose, Sony, Denon, Marantz, JBL, and we also offer customized products and solutions to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our mission at Sound Oasis is to provide customers with the best products and services, as well as the most competitive prices. Our professional team has extensive industry experience and expertise, and can provide customers with professional advice and solutions. We are committed to becoming a leading enterprise in the sales and service of audio and video products, and to providing customers with a first-class product and service experience.
