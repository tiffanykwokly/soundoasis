常見問題Frequently Asked Questions

  1. 退貨政策Return policy
    • 如需退貨，請在收到商品後的7天內聯繫客服，並提供訂單號碼、商品名稱和退貨原因。
    • 退貨商品必須處於未開封、未使用、完好無損的狀態，且附有原包裝、配件和說明書。
    • 退貨商品的運費由客戶自行承擔。

    • If you need to return an item, please contact customer service within 7 days of receiving the product and provide the order number, product name and reason for return.
    • The returned item must be in unopened, unused, and undamaged condition with original packaging, accessories and manual.
    • The shipping cost for returning the item will be borne by the customer.

  2. 換貨政策Exchange policy
    • 如需換貨，請在收到商品後的7天內聯繫客服，並提供訂單號碼、商品名稱和換貨原因。
    • 換貨商品必須處於未開封、未使用、完好無損的狀態，且附有原包裝、配件和說明書。
    • 換貨商品的運費由客戶自行承擔。

    • If you need to exchange an item, please contact customer service within 7 days of receiving the product and provide the order number, product name and reason for exchange.
    • The exchanged item must be in unopened, unused, and undamaged condition with original packaging, accessories and manual.
    • The shipping cost for exchanging the item will be borne by the customer.

  3. 異常商品
    • 如收到異常商品（如破損、缺陷等），請在收到商品後的7天內聯繫客服，並提供訂單號碼、商品名稱和異常情況的相關照片。
    • 經核實後，我們將為您提供免費退換貨服務。

    • If you receive a defective item (e.g. damaged, defective, etc.), please contact customer service within 7 days of receiving the product and provide the order number, product name and relevant photos of the defective item.
    • After verification, we will provide you with free return and exchange services.

以上退換貨政策僅供參考，影音狂熱保留最終決定權。
The above return and exchange policy is for reference only, Sound Oasis reserves the right of final decision.
